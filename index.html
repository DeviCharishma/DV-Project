Analysis of EV Charging Infrastructure in USA

PowerBI Dashboard

This dashboard provides an overview of EV charging infrastructure across the United States, showing distribution, usage patterns, and growth trends.

What the Map Shows

Most charging stations are in cities, while rural areas have very few. Places with government support get stations faster.

Usage Patterns

Highway stations are busiest during rush hours. City stations get more weekend use. Luxury brand stations often sit empty.

Key Problem

We need more stations in neighborhoods and rural areas to help everyone switch to EVs, not just city drivers.
